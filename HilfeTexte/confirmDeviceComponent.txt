request devicecomponent [param]

param: (-h|help) | (id=<id of Device>) | ((-u |unitid=)<id of unit>) | ((-n |name=)"<name>")

	-h|help: 					Shows this help

	id=<id of DeviceComponent>:	Confirm the DeviceComponent with the given ID

	(-u |unitid=)<id of unit>:	Assign the given Unit to the confirmed DeviceComponent

	(-n |name=)"<name>":			Assign the given Name to the confirmed DeviceComponent
