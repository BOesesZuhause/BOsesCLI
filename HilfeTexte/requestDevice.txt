request device [param]

param: (-a|all) | (-h|help) | (id=<id of Device>) | ((-z |zoneid=)<id of zone>) | ((-C |connectorid=)<id of connector>)

	-a|all: 								Lists all Devices

	-h|help:								Shows this help

	id=<id of Device>:						Displays the Device with the given ID

	(-z |zoneid=)<id of zone>:				Displays all Devices of the given Zone

	(-C |connectorid=)<id of connector>:	Displays all Devices of the given Connector
