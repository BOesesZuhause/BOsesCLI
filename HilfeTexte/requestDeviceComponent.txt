request devicecomponent (deviceid=<id of device>) [param]

	deviceid=<id of device>:	Displays DeviceComponents of the given Device

param: (-a|all) | (-h|help) | (id=<id of DeviceComponent>)

	-a|all: 				Lists all DeviceComponents

	-h|help: 				Shows this help

	id=<id of DeviceComponent>:		Displays the DeviceComponent with the given ID
