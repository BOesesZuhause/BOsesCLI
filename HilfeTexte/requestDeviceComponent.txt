request devicecomponent [param]

param: (-a|all) | (-h|help) | (id=<id of Device>) | ((-D |deviceid=)<id of device>)

	-a|all: 						Lists all DeviceComponents

	-h|help: 						Shows this help

	id=<id of DeviceComponent>:		Displays the DeviceComponent with the given ID

	(-D |deviceid=)<id of device>:	Displays all DeviceComponents of the given Device
