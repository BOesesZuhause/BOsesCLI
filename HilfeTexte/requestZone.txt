request zone [param]

param: (-a|all) | (-h|help) | (id=<id of zone>) | ((-s |superzoneid=)<id of superzone>)

	-a|all: 								Lists all Zones

	-h|help: 								Shows this help

	id=<id of zone>:						Displays the Zone with the given ID

	(-s |superzoneid)=<id of superzone>:	Displays all Zones of the given SuperZone
