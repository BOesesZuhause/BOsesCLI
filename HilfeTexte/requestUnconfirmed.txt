request unconfirmed [param]

param: (-a|all) | (-h|help) | (-C|connector) | (-d|devicecomponent) | (-D|device)

	-a|all: 			Lists all unconfirmed objects

	-h|help: 			Shows this help

	-C|connector:		Shows all unconfirmed connectors
	
	-d|devicecomponent:	Shows all unconfirmed devicecomponents
	
	-D|device:			Shows all unconfirmed devices
